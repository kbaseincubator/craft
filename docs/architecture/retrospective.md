# Augmentation stream retrospective

CRAFT is the **product shape** of three skills that came out of
the BERIL drop-in augmentation stream. This page is the
stream-level retrospective: what the augmentation framing
proved, why three of the four skills became CRAFT, and why
atlas stayed separate.

The full retrospective lives at
`AUGMENTATION-STREAM-RETROSPECTIVE.md` in the platform repo and
is included verbatim below. It is the dev-process counterpart to
the [platform proposal](../reference/platform-proposal.md) —
the proposal asks "should these become a platform"; the
retrospective documents "why this stream worked".

For product-facing architecture see [Platform
structure](platform-structure.md), [Skill
relationships](relationships.md), and the [cross-skill
contract](contract.md).
